Sudeep

Sudeep Sanjeev (born 2 September 1971), known mononymously as Sudeep, is an Indian film actor, director, producer, screenwriter, television presenter and singer, who primarily works in Kannada language films, in addition to appearances in Telugu, Hindi and Tamil language films
